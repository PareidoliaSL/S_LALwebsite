ART | WRITEPIECE | MUSIC

Pen Name : thepareidolia

I like depth. I like when I can look at something, and feel it in my heart. I like when I read a sentence with a few words and feel like I am drowning. I am not a masochist or a sadist, I just enjoy the quiet reminders that – My heart does more than pump blood. I call myself “ThePareidolia”. If you ask why so? You will listen me tell- “Be artistic. If you find the way the light falls through your window, and onto your bedroom walls pretty, write about it. Paint it. Sing about it. Well… I call it 'soft and golden as sunlit honey'! If it makes you glad to be alive, then it isn’t silly. You look for the beauty in things, be proud of it! Say the heavy rain that is kissing you. Write about The glow of moon.The dancing flowers. Make your world magical. Collect your meataphors and treasure them.”

DO WORDS HAVE POWER?

WORDS in my humble opinion, are the most inexhaustible source if magic we have. Words mean more than what is set down on paper. It takes the human voice to infuse them with deeper meaning. Ask anyone, who has ever been bullied, or been told, “NO” or has been told, “You are worthless!” Then ask anyone who has been told, “You are cured” or been told, “ You are my Hero” or even “I Love You”. Do you not think Words have power?

You may choose your words like a connoisseur, and polish it up like art. But the word that sways, and stirs and stays is the word that comes from the heal. If you constantly hear the same words, long enough, you start to believe them. Use your words wisely and speak things into beings that are positive. Make every felloe human feel they are all each dynamic, awesome and deserve to be done good and become good. Be very sure, to taste your words before you spit them out on someone. A tongue certainly might not have bones, but is the strongest; enough to break someone’s heart.

Words can be very powerful. Why not to use them to lift someone up, instead to knock them down. Once you realize the power, that you have, the influence you have just by using your words , you will think about what you say and what you speak. I strongly believe each of us can use our words, choose our words, to lift everyone up, to encourage and to inspire. We all have power. It’s what we do with it that makes is who we are. Kind words though short and easy to speak, their echos are truly endless. The power of words can have one in tears, evoke absolute joy, or lead one in action. There are words of encouragement, of sympathy, of love, and admiration. The right words can give one strength, define one’s faith, give flight to things that only live in one’s imagination. Words will inspire you, cut you, bring you back to life. They will comfort you in your time of need. They will comfort your soul.

Each of us shall watch our words, because they are free; Its how we use them that may cost us.
